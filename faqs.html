What are the basic rules of ludo?

Ludo is one of the easiest and most popular board games worldwide. Typically, a ludo game consists of a board, colored tokens, and dice. The rules of the game are relatively simple. The ludo players have to choose their colored tokens and compete against one another. The tokens start moving after a player gets six after rolling a dice. Please remember that for the initiation process of the tokens, you need a six each time, which you have to get by rolling the dice. The game allows the players to capture others' tokens and block them. The game aims to make your tokens reach the winning point before others, and the first player who can do so is declared to be the winner of the game.

What is Poker?

Poker is one of the most popular card games, which has been played worldwide for quite some time now. Typically, a game of poker can be played by up to ten players, and this game is typically played with a deck consisting of 52 cards. A few features of a poker game found in almost all poker variants are betting and bluffing, which determines the winner of a poker match. Besides, there is a strict ranking system that one needs to follow in poker.

Is poker legal in India?

Yes, poker is legal in India. However, poker's legality has been subject to various discussions and interpretations in the past few decades in India. Quoting a 1967 judgment, the Supreme Court of India reiterated in 1996 that the game requires considerable skills, and hence the game of poker is considered a skill-based game. It has also been observed that any game based on skills cannot be prohibited, as they are protected by the fundamental rights of the Indian citizens. The Karnataka and Calcutta high courts have recently argued that law enforcement has no role in poker, as it falls outside their judicial ambit. Hence, it is legal to play poker in India.
